The Artist

Chris
Marchese

Chris Marchese is a Toronto-based visual artist whose practice lives at the collision point of luxury fashion aesthetics, anime culture, and 3D mixed-media technique. His signature: hand-painted canvases and limited-edition art objects — sealed in high-gloss resin with real diamond dust — that have sold from $300 to $23,000.

He works alone. No studio assistants. No outsourced production. Every piece that leaves his studio is his — every detail, every layer, every grain of diamond dust applied by hand while the resin is still tacky.

The FIFA × Marchese collection is his most ambitious project to date — nine pieces made for one specific, unrepeatable moment in Toronto's history.
